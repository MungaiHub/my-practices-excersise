About Monkey Bay

Welcome to Monkey Bay, a social media platform designed to provide a fun and engaging experience for sharing videos. Our platform allows users to scroll through short video clips in a format similar to TikTok. The videos automatically play when snapped into view, thanks to the integration of JavaScript functionality, enhancing the user experience.

While we focused on using HTML and CSS, we faced challenges such as working with an emerald green theme and including a JavaScript feature to make videos play only when visible. Through this project, we gained a deeper understanding of web technologies like CSS, HTML, and JavaScript, allowing us to create a more functional and visually appealing platform.

Team Achievements: Our group learned the importance of collaboration and the balance between design and functionality. This project helped us develop a more intuitive user interface while also enhancing our coding skills.
